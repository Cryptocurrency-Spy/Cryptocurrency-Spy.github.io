What caused the crypto boom in 2017? In 2017, the cryptocurrency ecosystem was dominated by individual retail investors, many of whom were attracted to bitcoin's scarcity and the fact that it stood outside the global financial system.
The 2018 cryptocurrency crash was the sell-off of most cryptocurrencies starting in January 2018. After an unprecedented boom in 2017, the price of Bitcoin fell by about 65% from 6 January to 6 February 2018. Subsequently, nearly all other cryptocurrencies followed Bitcoin's crash.
